❓ How to Play

🎯 Your Mission

Run Rossi's Pizzeria through 12 months of rising inflation. Keep the business alive by managing costs, keeping customers happy, and keeping Marco motivated.

🔄 Each Month

1
Read the event — something has changed. Think about what it means.
2
Make your decision. Every choice has consequences — some won't appear until later.
3
Watch your gauges respond. Then click "Next Month".

⚠️ Cumulative Effects

This game has memory. Raising prices repeatedly damages Customer Trust permanently. Absorbing costs repeatedly drains Cash Reserves. Refusing Marco's wages repeatedly breaks his morale beyond recovery. Watch the three stress bars at the top carefully.

🟢 Core vs 🌟 Extension Mode

Core: Concepts explained in simple story language. Focus on the experience.

Extension: Economic vocabulary appears during gameplay — PED, wage-price spiral, real vs nominal, cost-push inflation. Aimed at stretch or GCSE taster.
