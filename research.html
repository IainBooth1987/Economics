How to use these resources
Two proven approaches for the classroom

These interactive tools have been designed to work at both ends of a lesson. Evidence from cognitive science supports two particularly effective applications:

Starter activity

Use before introducing the theory. Students explore the simulator without prior knowledge, forming intuitions and questions. The theory that follows has something to anchor to — making it far more memorable.

Plenary activity

Use after teaching the concept. Students apply their learning in a low-stakes, interactive context — consolidating understanding, identifying gaps, and testing whether they can transfer theory to a dynamic situation.
