Exam Tips

On PPP questions: Always explain why the gap exists (price level differences, non-traded goods, basket composition). Don't just state the number.

On exchange rate & trade: Use the Marshall-Lerner condition — a depreciation only improves the current account if PEDX + PEDM > 1.

On managed floats: China (CNY) is a favourite case study. Know the mechanism: PBOC buys USD to keep CNY weak → cheaper exports.

Common error: Confusing nominal and real exchange rates. PPP gives the real rate; market rate is nominal.
