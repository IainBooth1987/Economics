That's the point.

BOT-OMICS is a Socratic tutor. Instead of explaining concepts to you, it asks questions that guide you to work them out yourself.

Research consistently shows that retrieving information from memory is far more effective for long-term learning than re-reading notes or watching explanations. Every time you struggle to produce an answer, you strengthen the neural pathway that holds it.

Ask about any A-Level Economics topic — a concept you don't understand, a diagram you can't explain, an essay question you're unsure about. The tutor will respond with a question, not an answer. Keep going until you can explain it fully in your own words.

That's when it's yours.
