Master the four key liquidity ratios through theory, ranking activities, decision simulations and forecasting challenges.

Liquidity ratios measure a firm's ability to meet its short-term financial obligations using its current assets. A firm can be profitable but still fail if it cannot pay its debts when they fall due. Click each ratio card to explore the full theory.

Ratio 1
Current Ratio
Current Assets ÷ Current Liabilities
Ratio 2
Acid Test Ratio
(Current Assets − Inventory) ÷ Current Liabilities
Ratio 3
Receivables Days
(Receivables ÷ Sales) × 365
Ratio 4
Payables Days
(Payables ÷ Purchases) × 365
