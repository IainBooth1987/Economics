How to Play

Objective

Start with £10,000 in virtual money and grow your portfolio by trading stocks. React to news events, manage risk through diversification, and try to maximise your final portfolio value.

Controls

  • Start Market — opens setup to choose round length and enter your name, then begins trading
  • Pause Market — freezes prices so you can analyse and trade calmly
  • Reset Game — returns to £10,000 starting position
  • You can always trade — whether the market is running or paused
  • Role Card — you will be assigned a secret investor role with a bonus objective. Try to achieve it!
  • Timer — when the round ends, trading locks and your result screen appears

Market Events

  • Market Crash — a macro shock drives all prices down sharply
  • Market Boom — positive macro news lifts the whole market
  • Company News — positive or negative events affect individual stocks
  • PESTLE events — political, economic and technological changes drive volatility

Key Business Concepts

  • Shareholder value — rising share prices benefit shareholders; falling prices cause pressure on management
  • Diversification — spreading investment across sectors reduces risk. Watch your Diversification Score!
  • Stakeholder conflict — news events often pit short-term shareholder returns against other stakeholders
  • External environment — economic shocks (interest rates, GDP, regulation) affect the whole market

Trading Tips

  • Use the sparkline charts on each stock to spot price trends
  • Pause after a major event to review and rebalance your portfolio
  • Don't over-concentrate in one sector — spread your risk
  • Watch for news items with large % impacts — these create the biggest opportunities
