BIZ-BOT is a Socratic tutor. Choose how you want to be guided.

BizProblem

I won't explain it.
I'll ask until you can.

Tell BIZ-BOT which concept, model, framework, or topic you are finding difficult. It will respond with a question, not an answer.

Research consistently shows that retrieving information from memory is far more effective for long-term learning than re-reading notes. Every time you struggle to produce an answer, you strengthen the neural pathway that holds it.

Keep going until you can explain the concept fully in your own words. That's when it's yours.

BizQuestion

Paste your question.
I'll break it down with you.

Type or paste an exam question below. BIZ-BOT will identify the command term, the assessment objectives being tested, and interpret what the question is actually asking.

It will then guide you towards a strong answer through a series of probing questions — drawing the argument out of you, rather than giving it to you.

Once you have written a paragraph or attempt, paste it in at any point. BIZ-BOT will read your reasoning, identify the most significant gap in your chain of argument, and ask you a question to help you develop it — without telling you what to write.
