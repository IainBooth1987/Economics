Learning Objectives
Students should be able to identify the four phases of the business cycle (Boom, Recession, Slump, Recovery); explain how output gaps, employment, confidence and spending vary across each phase; and analyse how businesses and policymakers respond differently at each stage.

    Boom — above-trend growth
    Recession — GDP falling
    Slump — GDP at trough
    Recovery — GDP rising again
    ~ Adjacent phase also accepted
